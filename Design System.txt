Color System

Secondary Colors: 
Charcoal Gray #2E2E2E — symbolic of the product, gives contrast and solidity.
Warm Sand #F4D29A — natural, balancing, and brings warmth to the blue-gray palette.
Accent Colors:
Lime Green #9FD356 — represents sustainability and growth.
Amber Glow #FFB347 — evokes fire and energy, ideal for call-to-action highlights.
